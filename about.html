About

Skewers is a kebab barbecue food truck based in the Great Salt Lake area. It was started in 2005 by three friends: Stacey, Alex, and Hayden. While studying business at the University of Utah, they wanted to get their hands dirty and put their knowledge of running a business to work.

At this barbecue styled food truck you can choose from a variety of meats, vegetables, and fruits. Our menu includes our specialty combinations or you can get creative and make your own! With our online ordering system, getting lunch and dinner on your break is quick and easy.
